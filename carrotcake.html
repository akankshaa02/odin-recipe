Recipe for Carrot cake

Carrot cake is a type of cake that includes grated carrots in the batter, which gives it a moist and slightly dense texture. It often contains other ingredients like nuts, raisins, and spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. The cake is usually topped with a sweet and tangy cream cheese frosting, which complements the earthy flavors of the carrots.

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • extract 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
