Recipe for lasagna

lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is made by layering wide, flat pasta noodles with a variety of other ingredients, such as tomato sauce, cheese, and meat. The dish is typically baked in the oven, which allows the layers to meld together and become deliciously rich and flavorful.

  • Lasagna noodles (approximately 12 noodles)
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 1 pound of minced chicken or paneer
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 2 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
